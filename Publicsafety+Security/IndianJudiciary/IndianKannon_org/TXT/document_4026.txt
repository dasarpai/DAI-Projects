ORDER:
In proceedings of the Committee on August 18, 2009, settlement has been offered to the petitioner. Counsel for the petitioner states that the amount claimed is not as per the policy of the Financial Corporation. He wishes to withdraw this writ petition to lay challenge to the Committee proceedings, as mentioned above, and also to claim benefit as per policy of the Financial Corporation.
Dismissed as withdrawn with liberty to file fresh one, as prayed.
( Jasbir Singh ) Judge August 31, 2009.
