Allahabad High Court
Mahesh Chand Pandey vs Sri Gaurav Dayal, Collector, ... on 14 July, 2010
Court No. - 10

Case :- CONTEMPT APPLICATION (CIVIL) No. - 3189 of 2010

Petitioner :- Mahesh Chand Pandey
Respondent :- Sri Gaurav Dayal, Collector, Jaunpur & Anr.
Petitioner Counsel :- Dan Bahadur Yadav

Hon'ble Vikram Nath,J.
Heard the learned counsel for the applicant. It is alleged that the order dated 4.8.2009 passed by this Court has been violated. From a perusal of the petition, a prima facie case is made out. Issue notices to opposite parties fixing 27.8.2010. The opposite parties need not appear at this stage.
The counter affidavit may be filed within the aforesaid period or else charges may be framed after summoning the noticee. However, one more opportunity is granted to the opposite parties to comply with the order within a month.
The office may send a copy of this order along with the notices. Order Date :- 14.7.2010 pk
