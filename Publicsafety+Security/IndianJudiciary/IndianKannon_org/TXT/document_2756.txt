Rajasthan High Court - Jodhpur
Rajendra Kumar vs Nagar Palika, Bhadra & Ors on 2 February, 2009
S.B.Civil Writ Petition No.5254/08 Rajendra Kumar vs. Nagar Palika & ors.
Date of Order:- 2.2.2009 HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE PRAKASH TATIA Mr. Vijay Purohit for the petitioner. Mr. RS Choudhary for the respondents.
Heard learned counsel for the parties. The petitioner is aggrieved against the order dated 15.5.2008 byl which his two applications have been rejected by the trial court and plaintiff's permission to produce the copy of the map was denied and his prayer for summoning the map from the Municipal Board was also rejected.
After going through the facts of the case and the contents of the applications, it is not a fit case for exercise of jurisdiction under Article 227 of the Constitution of India in said order. Therefore, the writ petition is not entertained. However , the petitioner shall be free to challenge the said order dated 15.5.2008 in appeal in case any occasion arises for the petitioner to do so.
The writ petition is, therefore, dismissed with above liberty.
(PRAKASH TATIA)J mlt.
