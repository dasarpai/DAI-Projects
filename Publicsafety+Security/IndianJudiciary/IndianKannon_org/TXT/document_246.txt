                    J U D G M E N T
The petitioner claims appointment under the Kerala State Electricity Board through the Public Service Commission to the post of Mazdoor. The ranked list has already expired as evident from the fact that it was published on 15/06/2002. Therefore, the writ petition is dismissed.
