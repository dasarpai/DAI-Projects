ORAL
ORDER
Rule.
Learned advocate Mr. B.A.Shaikh for learned advocate Mr.Sukhwani appearing for the respondent waives service of notice of Rule.
Heard learned advocate Ms.Acharya appearing for the applicants and learned advocate Mr.B.A.Shaikh for learned advocate Mr.Sukhwani appearing for the respondent.
The application is taken out by applicant-panchayat who was by the order dated 4/3/2010 directed to deposit the amount as per the Trial Court's order.
Present application is taken out seeking extension of time for depositing the amount as directed by the Court.
Learned advocate Ms.Acharya for the applicants has submitted that the principal amount which was to be deposited has already been deposited, however, for want of funds, the applicants could not deposit the interest amount. Therefore extension of time is requested for. She has also submitted that the amount of interest comes to about rupees eight lakhs. She further submitted that now the cheque for the payable amount is ready and if the extension to deposit the amount is given the same will be deposited immediately.
Learned advocate Mr B.A.Shaikh for learned advocate Mr.Sukhwani has submitted that the opponent has no objection if the extension of time is granted.
Having regard to the aforesaid submissions and the request for time to deposit the balance amount, the application is allowed. The time to deposit the balance amount is extended until 30/10/2010. Rule is made absolute accordingly.
(K.M.THAKER, J.) (ila)     Top
