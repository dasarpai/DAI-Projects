DATED THIS THE 6TH DAY OF APRIL, 2010 O R D E R Petitioner, second respondent and their counsel are present. The parties have agreed to settle the main dispute accepting the C1(a) plan on the trial side which is appended to the decree in the second appeal 979/1973. Parties also agree that the property may be measured and divided inaccordance with that plan by the Taluk Surveyor. The Taluk Surveyor Cherthala is directed to survey and demarcate the property inaccordance with Ext.C1(a) plan and file a report before this Adalat. Parties will produce all necessary records along with a copy of this order before the Taluk Surveyor for compliance. Post on 27.05.2010.
