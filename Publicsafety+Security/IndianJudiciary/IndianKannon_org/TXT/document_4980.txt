HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE D.H.WAGHELA) After the petition being heard at some length, and after appearance of learned counsel Mr.N.V.Anjaria for respondent No.2 and Mr.A.J.Desai for respondent No.1, the petition was proposed to be withdrawn with a view to challenging the election before the appropriate forum at the appropriate time.
