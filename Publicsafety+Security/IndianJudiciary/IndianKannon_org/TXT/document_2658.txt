Allahabad High Court
Sabir Husain vs State Of U.P. Thru' Principal ... on 14 July, 2010
Chief Justice's Court

Case :- WRIT - C No. - 40190 of 2010

Petitioner :- Sabir Husain
Respondent :- State Of U.P. Thru' Principal Secy. Land &
Revenue & Ors.
Petitioner Counsel :- Krishna Kumar Singh
Respondent Counsel :- C.S.C.

Hon'ble Ferdino Inacio Rebello,Chief Justice
Hon'ble Amreshwar Pratap Sahi,J.
Heard learned counsel for the petitioner.
The dispute raised in this petition is that some obstruction is being made by respondent nos.5 and 6 in relation to some construction being raised by the petitioner. In our opinion, against such grievance, the remedy available to the petitioner is to file a civil suit before appropriate court.
With the above observation, petition stands dismissed.
Order Date :- 14.7.2010 VMA (F.I. Rebello, C.J.) (A.P. Sahi, J.)
