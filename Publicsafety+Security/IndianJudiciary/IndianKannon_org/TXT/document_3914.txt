Patna High Court - Orders
Dilip Kumar & Anr vs State Of Bihar & Anr on 19 July, 2011
          IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                   CR.MISC. NO.36859 OF 2008
                      DILIP KUMAR & ANR
                            VERSUS
                     STATE OF BIHAR & ANR
                             *******
2 19/07/2011 Learned Counsel for the petitioners submits that the trial in this case has commenced and all prosecution witnesses except the official witnesses have been examined in this case and as such, this application has become infructuous.
This application is dismissed as infructuous. However, the Trial Court is directed to expedite the trial of the case at the earliest.
Anand                                                     ( Sheema Ali Khan, J. )
