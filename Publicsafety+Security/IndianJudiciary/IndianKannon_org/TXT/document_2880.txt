Allahabad High Court
Hinchh Lal Dubey vs Board Of Revenue, U.P. & Others on 19 July, 2010
Court No. - 26

Case :- WRIT - A No. - 85 of 1996

Petitioner :- Hinchh Lal Dubey
Respondent :- Board Of Revenue, U.P. & Others
Petitioner Counsel :- Lalji Pandey
Respondent Counsel :- C.S.C.

Hon'ble Anil Kumar,J.
Sri Lalji Pandey, learned counsel for the petitioner submits that due to efflux of time , this writ petition has become infructuous , so the same may be dismissed as such.
The writ petition is dismissed as infructuous. Interim order, if any, stands vacated. Order Date :- 19.7.2010 dk/-
