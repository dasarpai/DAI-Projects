                                   JUDGMENT
The substantial challenge raised by the petitioner against Ext.P2 communication issued by the Principal Secretary, Land Revenue Department was accepted by this Court, at the instance of the similar claim in the judgment in Pasupathi Namboothiri v. Sate of Kerala [2008(4) KLT 163]. In the result, the Taluk Land Board shall proceed to consider the petitioner's claim in accordance with law.
Writ Petition is disposed of as above.
