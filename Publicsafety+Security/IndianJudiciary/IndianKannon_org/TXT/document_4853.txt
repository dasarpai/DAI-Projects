ORAL
ORDER
Rule.
Learned AGP Mr. H.H. Parikh waives service of Rule on behalf of the respondents.
Heard.
Mr. Parekh submits that he will approach the concerned Authority by way of representation and the concerned Authority may be directed to decide the said representation, in accordance with law, as early as possible. Learned AGP Mr. Parikh has no objection if necessary direction is given to the Authority.
In view of above, the petitioner may make the representation before the concerned Authority. On receipt of such representation the concerned Authority is directed to decide the same as per the policy of the Government and in accordance with law, as early as possible.
In view of above direction Mr. Parekh does not press this petition. Hence, this petition is disposed of as not pressed. Rule discharged. No order as to costs.
(Z.K.SAIYED,J.) sas
