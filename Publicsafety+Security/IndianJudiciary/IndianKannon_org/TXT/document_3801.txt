Allahabad High Court
Smt. Chandrawati And Others vs District Magistrate/Collector ... on 16 July, 2010
Court No. - 34

Case :- WRIT - C No. - 40544 of 2010

Petitioner :- Smt. Chandrawati And Others
Respondent :- District Magistrate/Collector Gautam Budh Nagar
Petitioner Counsel :- Ateeq Ahmad Khan
Respondent Counsel :- C.S.C.

Hon'ble Prakash Chandra Verma,J.
Hon'ble Ram Autar Singh,J.
The only grievance of the petitioners relates to demarcation of the Gata no. 1154 in village Nilauni Shahpur Pargana Dankaur, Tahsil Sadar, district Gautam Budh Nagar .
The Sub. Divisional Magistrate, Gautam Budh Nagar is directed to demarcate the Gata of the petitioner within a period of three months and allow the petitioner to enjoy the peaceful possession of his Gata of which he is abadi holder after taking evidence and perusal of the records.
With the aforesaid direction the writ petition is disposed of finally.
Order Date :- 16.7.2010 ssm
