Allahabad High Court
Bahadur Singh vs State Of U.P. & Others on 28 January, 2010
Court No. - 18

Case :- WRIT - A No. - 4085 of 2010

Petitioner :- Bahadur Singh
Respondent :- State Of U.P. & Others
Petitioner Counsel :- Prashant K. Lal Respondent Counsel :- C.S.C. Hon'ble Sudhir Agarwal, J.
Learned counsel for the petitioner at the outset stated that  on account of certain material facts not brought on record  he   may   be   permitted   to   withdraw   this   petition   with   a  liberty to file a fresh petition.  Learned Standing Counsel has no objection to the above  request. 
The writ petition is accordingly dismissed as withdrawn  with the aforesaid liberty.  Dt/­28.01.2010 AK­4085/10
