Section 39 in The Tripura Land Revenue And Land Reforms Act, 1960 No. 43 Of 1960
39. Additional assessment for water advantages. Notwithstanding anything contained in this Chapter, the Administrator may direct that any land in respect of which the rate of revenue has been determined shall be liable to be assessed to additional land revenue during, the term of the settlement for additional advantages accruing to it from water received on account of irrigation works or improvements in existing irrigation works' completed after the table of revenue- rates dame into force and not effected by or at the expense of the holder of the land.
