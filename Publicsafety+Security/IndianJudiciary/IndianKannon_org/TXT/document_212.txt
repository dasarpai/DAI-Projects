Allahabad High Court
Sayed Ali And Others. vs Shri Ram Chandra Saroj And Another on 12 July, 2010
Court No. - 3

Case :- CONTEMPT No. - 1405 of 2010

Petitioner :- Sayed Ali And Others.
Respondent :- Shri Ram Chandra Saroj And Another
Petitioner Counsel :- Ramesh Kumar

Hon'ble Shri Narayan Shukla,J.
Supplementary affidavit filed today is taken on record. The learned counsel for the petitioners submits that in violation of order dated 10th of May, 2002 passed by this Court in writ petition no. 1455(M/S) of 2002, the opposite parties have issued auction notice of the land in dispute, which is fixed on 13th of July, 2010. Therefore I hereby request to learned Additional Chief Standing counsel to call upon the opposite parties to appear before this court on 13th of July, 2010 and to show cause.
List on 13th of July, 2010.
Order Date :- 12.7.2010 Amit
