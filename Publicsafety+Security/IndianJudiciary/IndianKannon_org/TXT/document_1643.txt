Mr. Kamal Sehgal, Advocate for the respondents.
RAJAN GUPTA J. (ORAL) This is a petition under Section 482 Code of the Criminal Procedure seeking quashing of complaint No. 33 dated 3rd April, 2008 (Annexure P-5), under Sections 14(2) & 14A(1) of the Provident fund & Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952 and also orders, Annexures P-6(i) and P-6(ii), passed by Sub Divisional Judicial Magistrate, Samana, whereby notice of the complaint was issued to the petitioners.
Learned counsel for the petitioners submits that during the pendency of this petition, the accused/petitioners have been convicted by the trial court, therefore, the present petition has been rendered infructuous. He, therefore, prays that he may be allowed to withdraw the instant petition.
Learned counsel for the respondents has not disputed the factual averments that the petitioners that the petitioners have been convicted by the trial court during the pendency of the present petition and submits that he has no objection if the petition is allowed to be withdrawn.
Resultantly, the instant petition is dismissed as withdrawn.
