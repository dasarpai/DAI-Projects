                                JUDGMENT
The petitioner seeks an out of turn expeditious disposal of a matter filed before the State Election Commission. The materials on record do not disclose the requirement of any order by this court in that regard. It is submitted by the counsel for the election commission that the matter filed by the petitioner before the election commission and referred to in the writ petition will be disposed of within a period of five months from now. This is recorded. The writ petition is closed.
THOTTATHIL B RADHAKRISHNAN, JUDGE lgk/6/10
