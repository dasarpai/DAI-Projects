Allahabad High Court
Sita Ram Tripathi vs State Of U.P. & Others on 13 July, 2010
Court No. - 38

Case :- WRIT - A No. - 3936 of 1993

Petitioner :- Sita Ram Tripathi
Respondent :- State Of U.P. & Others
Petitioner Counsel :- B.K.Singh Raghubanshi
Respondent Counsel :- S.C.

Hon'ble Shishir Kumar,J.
This case is listed under the Heading 'Old Cases'. In spite of repeated calls, no one appears to press this writ petition on merits.
Therefore, the writ petition is hereby dismissed. Interim order, if any, stands discharged. No order is passed as to costs. Order Date :- 13.7.2010 V.Sri/-
