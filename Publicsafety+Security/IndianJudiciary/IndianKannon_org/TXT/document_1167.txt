                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA

2 01.07.2008. This application has been filed for restoration of Cr.Misc.No.50161 of 2007 which dismissed for non prosecution by order dated 9.5.2008.
Sufficient cause has been shown for non appearance in paragraph 3 and 4 of the petition. Accordingly this application is allowed and Cr/Misc.No.50161 of 2007 is restored to its original file.
(Mridula Mishra, J) Sss/-
