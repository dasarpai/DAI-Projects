Central Government Act
Section 13 in The National Bank For Agriculture And Rural Development (Amendment) Act 2000
13. Substitution of new section for section 26.- For section 26 of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted, namely:-" 26. Purchase and sale of shares.- The National Bank may subscribe to, or purchase or sell stocks, shares, bonds or debentures of, or invest in the securities of, any institution or class of institutions concerned with agriculture and rural development which the Board may approve subject to such terms and conditions as it may deem fit.".
