Allahabad High Court
Jeet Singh & Others vs D.D.C. & Others on 5 January, 2010
Court No. - 5

Case :- WRIT - B No. - 71280 of 2009

Petitioner :- Jeet Singh & Others
Respondent :- D.D.C. & Others
Petitioner Counsel :- Pradeep Kumar Rai
Respondent Counsel :- C.S.C.

Hon'ble Sabhajeet Yadav,J.
Sri Santosh Srivastava has filed Vakalatnama on behalf of respondents no. 4 to 7. He is directed to file counter affidavit within four weeks. Petitioners shall have three weeks thereafter to file rejoinder affidavit.
List immediately thereafter.
As interim measure, till the next date of listing in case the chak has been carved out in favour of petitioners, they shall not be dispossessed therefrom.
