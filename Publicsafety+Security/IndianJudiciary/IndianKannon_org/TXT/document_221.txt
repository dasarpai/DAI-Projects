Allahabad High Court
Mohammad Yasin vs Pradeep Shukla, Prin. Secy. ... on 6 August, 2010
Court No. - 3

Case :- CONTEMPT No. - 1339 of 2010

Petitioner :- Mohammad Yasin
Respondent :- Pradeep Shukla, Prin. Secy. Medical & Health And Another
Petitioner Counsel :- Syed Mohd. Munis Jafari

Hon'ble Shri Narayan Shukla,J.
Counter affidavit filed on behalf opposite party No.1 is taken on record, whereby the decision on the petitioner's representation has been brought on record, upon perusal of which being satisfied with the action of the opposite parties, I hereby dismiss the contempt petition and discharge the notices issued to the opposite parties.
Order Date :- 6.8.2010 Banswar
