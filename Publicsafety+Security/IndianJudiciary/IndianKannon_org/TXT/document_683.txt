7 28.10.2010 Let office make a fresh stamp report with regard to maintainability of these civil revisions in view of the provisions as contained in Order XLIII Rule 1(k) as well as the decision of this Court in the case of Sachida Nand Yadav & others v. Dinesh Yadav & others (1991 BBCJ, 511) and the decisions of the Apex Court in the cases of Shahazada Bi v. Halimabi(since deceased) (AIR 2004 Supreme Court, 3942), K. Rudrappa v. Shivappa(AIR 2004 Supreme Court, 4346) and Madan Naik v. Mst. Hansubala Devi & others (AIR 1983 Supreme Court, 676).
Put up these matters on 25.11.2010 under the same heading maintaining its position.
