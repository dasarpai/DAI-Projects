Allahabad High Court
Rakesh Kumar Mishra vs Kailash Bharti & 3 Ors. on 2 July, 2010
Court No. - 3

Case :- CONTEMPT No. - 204 of 2003

Petitioner :- Rakesh Kumar Mishra
Respondent :- Kailash Bharti & 3 Ors.
Petitioner Counsel :- B.R. Singh
Respondent Counsel :- Lalit Shukla

Hon'ble Shri Narayan Shukla,J.
Case is called out. None appears for the petitioner to press this contempt petition.
Learned Standing Counsel on the basis of the instruction submits that the order passed by this court in on 11.7.2001 in writ petition no. 457 (cons) of 2001 has been complied with. The contempt petition is dismissed. The notice issued to opposite party no.1 is hereby discharged.
Order Date :- 2.7.2010 GSY
