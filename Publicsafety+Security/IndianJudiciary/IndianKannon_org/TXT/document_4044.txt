                            JUDGMENT
Kurian Joseph,J.
Petitioner is aggrieved by the interim order for maintenance granted by the Family Court for maintenance granted by the Family Court. According to the petitioner the order is passed on facts which are neither pleaded nor contended before the Family Court. It is for the petitioner to move the Family Court pointing out such alleged factual mistakes, if any, committed by the Family Court.
The writ petition is disposed of as above..
(KURIAN JOSEPH, JUDGE) (HARUN-UL-RASHID, JUDGE) ahg.
