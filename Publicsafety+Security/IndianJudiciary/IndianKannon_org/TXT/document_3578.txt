Rajasthan High Court - Jodhpur
Pukhraj vs Lrs Of Hari Singh on 1 September, 2022
Bench: Madan Gopal Vyas
HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN JODHPUR S.B. Civil Second Appeal No. 211/2005 Pukhraj
----Appellant Versus Lrs Of Hari Singh
----Respondent For Appellant(s) : Mr. Akshay Nagori For Respondent(s) : Mr. Rishabh Shrimali HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE MADAN GOPAL VYAS Order 01/09/2022 Matter comes up on an application under Section 151 CPC filed by learned counsel for the appellant for giving notices 'dasti' to him.
For the reasons mentioned in the application, the same is allowed.
Issue notice to the unserved respondents. Notices are made returnable within three weeks and be given 'dasti' to learned counsel for the appellant.
(MADAN GOPAL VYAS),J 58-Bharti/-
