                                  JUDGMENT
The petitioner is working as Lower Division Clerk, presently in Vattamkulam Grama Panchayath. He has submitted Ext.P3 application in the prescribed proforma seeking for orders of transfer to Kuttippuram Grama Panchayath or to the nearby Panchayaths like Thirunavaya or Thanoor. Ext.P2 representation has also been filed before the second respondent for the very same purpose.
2. Government Pleader submits that Ext.P3 application will be considered by the second respondent at the time of general transfers, 2007.
3. In the circumstances, the writ petition is disposed of directing the second respondent to consider and pass orders on Ext.P3 application in the prescribed proforma supplemented by Ext.P2 representation, as early as possible, at any rate, during general transfers in April/May of 2007.
