                                        ORDER
Revision petitioner is the complainant in the proceedings initiated under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act. The same was dismissed vide Section 204(4) of the Code of Criminal Procedure for lapse on the part of the complainant to initiate steps as ordered by the court below.
2. It is submitted that the petitioner had really taken steps. But the same was omitted to be brought to the notice of the court below. In the circumstances, the order of the court below is set aside. The court below is directed to permit the petitioner to further pursue the matter. Revision petitioner shall appear before the Judicial First Class Magistrate Court I, Kochi on 12.4.2007.
The criminal revision petition is disposed of as above.
K.R.UDAYABHANU, JUDGE csl
