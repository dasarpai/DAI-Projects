                          J U D G M E N T
The petitioner, a student of Mechanical Branch in B.Tech Degree in the S.N.G.C.E, Kadayirippu, is seeking for a direction to the respondent University to complete the revaluation of the papers of the petitioner and to inform the results thereon to him.
2. Learned Standing Counsel for the University submitted that the revaluation has already been completed and a memo has been issued to the petitioner on 24.12.2009 informing that there is no change in the result. The same is recorded and this writ petition is accordingly closed.
T.R. RAMACHANDRAN NAIR JUDGE smp
