ORAL
ORDER
1. Learned advocate for the petitioners Mr.Trivedi submits that the petitions are similar to the petitions being Special Civil Application Nos.10192 to 10204 of 2008, wherein this Hon'ble Court was pleased to issue notice on 08.08.2008 returnable on 26.08.2008 along with notice as to interim relief on the same day. He further submitted that the Court was pleased to protect the interest of the petitioners by granting ad interim relief in terms of para 6(b) of those petitions.
Those petitions were on board today and the matters are now adjourned to 15.09.2008 and ad interim relief granted earlier is ordered to continue till further orders.
2. NOTICE returnable on 15.09.2008. NOTICE as to interim relief returnable on the same day. Mr.Raval, learned Asst Solicitor General of India, waives service of notice on behalf of respondent No.1.
In the meantime, ad interim relief in terms of para 6(b).
Direct service is permitted.
