Rajasthan High Court - Jodhpur
K.K.Shah vs Union Of India & Ors on 27 November, 2009
  IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT JODHPUR
  --------------------------------------------------------


                    CIVIL WRIT No. 1265 of 2003

                                  K.K.SHAH
                              V/S
                         UNION OF INDIA & ORS

    Mr. KK SHAH, SELF PET.

    Mr. VK MATHUR, for the respondent

    Date of Order : 27.11.2009

                     HON'BLE SHRI N P GUPTA,J.

                             ORDER
-----
Learned counsel submits that the controversy involved in the present case has been decided by Hon'ble the Supreme Court against the petitioner in State of Punjab & Ors. Vs. Amar Nath Goyal reported in 2005(6) SCC 754, therefore, the writ petition no more survives. The same is, therefore, dismissed.
( N P GUPTA ),J.
