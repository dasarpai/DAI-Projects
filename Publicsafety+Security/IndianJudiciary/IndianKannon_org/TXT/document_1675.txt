Allahabad High Court
Mewa Lal vs D.D.C. & Others on 21 January, 2010
Court No. - 5

Case :- WRIT - B No. - 71373 of 2009

Petitioner :- Mewa Lal
Respondent :- D.D.C. & Others
Petitioner Counsel :- Dan Bahadur Yadav,Lalta Prasad
Respondent Counsel :- C.S.C.,Mahesh Narain Singh,S.C. Tripathi

Hon'ble Sabhajeet Yadav,J.
Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned Standing Counsel for respondent no.1 as well as Sri S.C. Tripathi appearing for respondent no.2. Sri Mahesh Narain Singh has put in appearance on behalf of respondent no.10. Issue notice to the respondents no.3 to 9 returnable at an early date. The respondents are directed to file counter affidavit within four weeks. The petitioner shall have three weeks thereafter to file rejoinder affidavit. List thereafter.
Till the next date of listing, parties are directed to maintain status quo on spot. Order Date :- 21.1.2010 LJ/-
