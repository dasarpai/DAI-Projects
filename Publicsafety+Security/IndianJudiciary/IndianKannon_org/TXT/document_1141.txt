JASBIR SINGH, J. (ORAL).
This writ petition has been filed to lay challenge to the order dated 08.09.2009 (P-6), passed by Debt Recovery Tribunal-II, Chandigarh, dismissing objections filed by the petitioners against auction of the mortgaged property.
Against the order under challenge, appeal lies. Writ petition is not competent.
Dismissed.
