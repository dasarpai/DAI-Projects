Allahabad High Court
Dr. Avinash Singh S/O Prof. Shree ... vs State Of U.P. Thru Prin. Secy. Home ... on 21 January, 2010
Court No. - 25

Case :- MISC. BENCH No. - 9747 of 2009

Petitioner :- Dr. Avinash Singh S/O Prof. Shree Singh
Respondent :- State Of U.P. Thru Prin. Secy. Home & Ors.
Petitioner Counsel :- S.M. Royekwar
Respondent Counsel :- G.A.

Hon'ble Abdul Mateen,J.
Hon'ble Vedpal,J.
Sri Akhilesh Singh, learned counsel appearing for opposite party no. 3 states that Dr. Sharda Singh since has given birth to a child on 22.11.2009 and due to post natal problem, she could not attend the court today and some other date be fixed since the doctor has advised her to take bed rest.
As prayed, list on 25.2.2010. On the said date Dr Sharda Singh shall appear before the court to apprise as to whether she is agreeable for sending the matter to the Mediation and Conciliation Centre of this Court or not.
Interim order granted earlier shall continue till the next date of listing.
Order Date :- 21.1.2010 Tripathi
