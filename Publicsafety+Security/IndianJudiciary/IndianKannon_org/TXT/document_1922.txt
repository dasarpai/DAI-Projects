Allahabad High Court
Jhinku Ram vs State Of U.P. & Others on 5 July, 2010
Court No. - 38

Case :- WRIT - A No. - 38184 of 2010

Petitioner :- Jhinku Ram
Respondent :- State Of U.P. & Others
Petitioner Counsel :- Anita Tripathi
Respondent Counsel :- C.S.C.

Hon'ble Shishir Kumar,J.
Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned Standing Counsel.
Petitioner was working on the post of Revenue Inspector, subsequently promoted to the post of Naib Tehildar in the year 1994. Petitioner after completion of eight years service, he was entitled for selection Grade from 2002. But the same has not been provided in spite of the repeated request and representations submitted by the petitoner. The last representation submitted by the petitioner is dated 26.11.2007. Petitioner retired from service in the year 2007, but Grade has not yet been provided to the petitioner.
I have considered the submission of the learned counsel for the petitioner and perused the record. In view of the facts and circumstances of the present case, this writ petition is being disposed of finally directing the respondents No.2 to consider the claim of the petitioner for providing selection Grade according to law on the basis of the representation submitted by the petitioner and may take decision within a period of three months from the date of production of certified copy of this order before him.
The writ petition is disposed of accordingly.
No order as to costs.
Order Date :- 5.7.2010 Pr/-
