Allahabad High Court
Manoj Kumar vs Jeevdhan And Another on 18 January, 2010
Court No. - 10

Case :- CONTEMPT APPLICATION (CIVIL) No. - 165 of 2010

Petitioner :- Manoj Kumar
Respondent :- Jeevdhan And Another
Petitioner Counsel :- J.P. Mishra

Hon'ble Vikram Nath,J.
It is alleged that the order dated 04.03.2009 passed by this court has been violated. From a perusal of the petition, a prima facie case is made out.
Issue notice to opposite parties fixing 4.3.2010. The opposite parties need not appear at this stage.
The counter affidavit may be filed within the aforesaid period or else charges may be framed after summoning the noticee. However, one more opportunity is granted to the opposite parties to comply with the order within a month.
The office may send a copy of this order along with the notice.
Order Date :- 18.1.2010 RPS
