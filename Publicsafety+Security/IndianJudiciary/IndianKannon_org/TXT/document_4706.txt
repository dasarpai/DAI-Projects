Allahabad High Court
Najmul Hasan Khan vs State Of U.P. & Others on 1 July, 2010
Court No. - 38

Case :- WRIT - A No. - 3877 of 1993

Petitioner :- Najmul Hasan Khan
Respondent :- State Of U.P. & Others
Petitioner Counsel :- S.Farman Ahmad Naqvi,S.A.S.Abidi
Respondent Counsel :- S.C.,U.N.Sharma

Hon'ble Shishir Kumar,J.
This case is listed under the Heading 'Old Cases'. In spite of repeated calls, no one appears to press this writ petition on merits.
Therefore, the writ petition is hereby dismissed. Interim order, if any, stands discharged. No order is passed as to costs. Order Date :- 1.7.2010 V.Sri/-
