Patna High Court - Orders
Arjun Das vs The State Of Bihar &Amp; Ors on 31 March, 2011
                IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                            CWJC No.12222 of 2004
                                ARJUN DAS
                                  Versus
                         THE STATE OF BIHAR & ORS
                                 -----------
08 31.03.2011 Let petitioner add Bihar School Examination Board, Patna as a party-respondent. Learned counsel for the petitioner would serve two copies of the writ petition along with counter affidavit and supplementary counter affidavit on the learned counsel who normally appears for the Bihar School Examination Board in this Court within a week and file receipt of service of copy within a week.
List this matter on 25th April, 2011 retaining its position by when learned counsel for the Bihar School Examination would file a counter affidavit in the matter as to the two stands taken in respect of petitioner's Teachers Training Certificate. While serving copy of the writ petition with annexures on the counsel for the Bihar School Examination Board learned counsel for the petitioner would also indicate about the order of this Court passed today.
     Trivedi/                             (Navaniti Prasad Singh, J.)
