Allahabad High Court
Sukhrani vs D.D.C. Sitapur And 5 Ors. on 18 January, 2010
Court No. - 3

Case :- CONSOLIDATION No. - 399 of 2008

Petitioner :- Sukhrani
Respondent :- D.D.C. Sitapur And 5 Ors.
Petitioner Counsel :- O.P.Mishra
Respondent Counsel :- C.S.C.,R.N.Gupta

Hon'ble Shri Narayan Shukla,J.
Application is allowed.
Let legal heir of sole petitioner who died on 16.8.2009, descriptions of whom have been given in the affidavit filed in support of this application be substituted in his place by way of incorporation in the memo of writ petition within a week. List the case in the next cause list showing the name of Mr. Satendra Nath Rai as counsel for the petitioner in the cause list. Application is allowed.
Delay is condoned.
Order Date :- 18.1.2010 GSY
