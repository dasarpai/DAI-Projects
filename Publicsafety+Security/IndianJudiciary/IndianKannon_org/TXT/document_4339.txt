                                       ORAL ORDER
2 03-02-2020 Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the counsel appearing on behalf of the State.
The petitioner is in custody in connection with Riga P.S. Case No. 284 of 2019 for the offences under Sections 354(B) of the Indian Penal Code and Section 10/18 POCSO Act, 2012.
Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the petitioner has no criminal antecedent and he is in custody since 20.10.2019. He further submits that there is delay of 20 days in lodging the F.I.R. and petitioner is aged about 65 years old.
Considering the fact that petitioner is aged about 65 years old, the Court is inclined to grant bail to the petitioner, let the petitioner, named above, be released on bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs.50,000/- (Rupees fifty thousand) with two Patna High Court CR. MISC. No.6207 of 2020(2) dt.03-02-2020 sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of Court of learned Additional District Judge-I, Special Judge (POCSO Act) Sitamarhi in connection with Riga P.S. Case No. 284 of 2019, subject to condition that one of the bailors should be female of the locality.
(Anil Kumar Upadhyay, J) mdrashid/-
