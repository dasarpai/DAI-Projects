Allahabad High Court
Anand Deo Pal And Another vs Deputy Director Of Consolidation ... on 18 January, 2010
Court No. - 5

Case :- WRIT - B No. - 1746 of 2010

Petitioner :- Anand Deo Pal And Another
Respondent :- Deputy Director Of Consolidation And Others
Petitioner Counsel :- S.P. Bharti
Respondent Counsel :- C.S.C.

Hon'ble Sabhajeet Yadav,J.
It is pointed out that Revision no.6 Pannal Pal Vs. Jagannath and others u/s 48 of U.P. C.H. Act is pending before Additional Collector/Deputy Director of Consolidation, Sant Kabir Nagar. It is pointed out by learned counsel for the petitioners that hearing of said case has already taken place on 2.01.2008 and date was fixed for 14.1.2008 but the case has yet not been decided.
In this view of the matter, the revisional court is directed to hear the matter afresh and decide the case expeditiously preferably within a period of two months from the date of production of certified copy of the order passed by this Court before him without granting any undue adjournment to either of the parties.
With the aforesaid observation and direction, writ petition stands disposed of.
Order Date :- 18.1.2010 SL
