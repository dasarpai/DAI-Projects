Patna High Court - Orders
Kedar Prasad Singh vs State Of Bihar & Ors. on 29 September, 2011
                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                           MJC No.2412 of 2010
                            Kedar Prasad Singh
                                  Versus
                          State Of Bihar & Ors.
                               -----------
3 29.09.2011 Heard learned counsels for the petitioner and the State.
A show cause has been filed on behalf of the opposite party no.1 stating therein that the order of the Court has been complied with by passing the order contained in memo no. 43 dated 07.11.2010 as contained in Annexure-C to the show cause.
Considering the submissions and the show cause, I am not inclined to proceed further in the matter. The MJC application accordingly stands disposed of. Manish/- ( Shailesh Kumar Sinha,J.)
