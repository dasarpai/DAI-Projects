Allahabad High Court
Tribhuvan Singh And Another vs State Of U.P.And Others on 7 July, 2010
Court No. - 52
Crl. Misc. Extension of Time Application No.182345 of 2010
In

Case :- APPLICATION U/S 482 No. - 33485 of 2009

Petitioner :- Tribhuvan Singh And Another
Respondent :- State Of U.P.And Others
Petitioner Counsel :- J.S.P. Singh,Ravish Kr. Singh
Respondent Counsel :- Govt. Advocate

Hon'ble Arvind Kumar Tripathi,J.
This is an application for extension of time for appearance and moving of bail application. The time was allowed on 29.4.2010 to appear and move bail application.
There is no good ground for extension of time. Expeditious disposal of application is right of the accused applicant.
Accordingly, application stand disposed off.
Order Date :- 7.7.2010 Pramod
