Patna High Court - Orders
Rameshwar Prasad @ Rameshwar Sao ... vs The State Of Bihar &Amp; Anr on 16 August, 2010
                  IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                             Cr.Misc. No.30374 of 2010
                 RAMESHWAR PRASAD @ RAMESHWAR SAO & ORS
                                       Versus
                           THE STATE OF BIHAR & ANR
                                      -----------
02. 16.08.2010 Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned Additional Public Prosecutor for the State.
This application has been filed seeking restoration of Cr. Misc. No. 747/2005 which was dismissed for non- prosecution vide order dated 30.07.2010. On going through the records and hearing of learned counsel, the order dated 30.07.2010 for the reasons recorded in the application stands recalled and Cr. Misc. No. 747/2005 is restored subject to payment of Rs. 500/- with the High Court Legal Services Committee.
Rajeev/                                                 (Akhilesh Chandra, J.)
