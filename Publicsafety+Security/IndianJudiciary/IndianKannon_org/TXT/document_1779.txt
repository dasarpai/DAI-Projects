                                         Decision i


1.     The complainant did not avail of the opportunity of personal hearing on
11/8/2008. The complaint is therefore examined on merit.


2.     The complainant has alleged that information asked for vide his
application dated 16.11.2007 have not been furnished to him by the CPIO of the
respondent.
3. The CPIO is directed to furnish the information within 15 working days from the date of issue of this decision, failing which penalty proceedings u/s 20(1) of the Act, would be initiated.
4. The complainant is advised to contact the concerned CPIO along with a copy of his RTI application for ready reference. He should ensure that the required information is clearly specified as per the provisions of Section 2(f) of the Act, which requires that information should be available in any material form.
i "If you don't ask, you don't get." - Mahatma Gandhi
5. The complaint is thus disposed of.
