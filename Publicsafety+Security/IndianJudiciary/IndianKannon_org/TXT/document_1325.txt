Punjab-Haryana High Court
Present : None For The vs Mr. Avnish Mittal on 1 September, 2009
                       R.S.A No. 2997 OF 2006.

                                 ******
Zile Singh and others vs Kaptan & others Present : None for the appellants.
Mr. Avnish Mittal, Advocate for respondents No.1 to 6.
******* Even on second call, no one has put in appearance on behalf of the appellants. The situation was no different on the last two occasions. In these circumstances, this appeal is dismissed for want of prosecution. Let a note be given in the cause list informing Shri Pritam Saini, Advocate for the appellants.
