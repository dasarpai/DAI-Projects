                             J U D G M E N T
The only request made by the petitioner in this writ petition is to direct the second respondent to consider Ext.P4 claim petition filed by the petitioner before him.
2. Now that Ext.P4 application has been filed, it is for the second respondent to consider the application in accordance with law and also adverting to Ext.P5, a judgment of the Apex Court in Ahmmedkutty Vs. Mariakutty Umma [2000 (1) KLT 829 (SC)]. This, the second respondent shall do with notice to the affected parties and as expeditiously as possible and at any rate, within a period of three months from the date of production of a copy of this judgment.
3. Petitioner shall produce a copy of this judgment and writ petition before the second respondent for compliance.
Writ petition is disposed of as above.
