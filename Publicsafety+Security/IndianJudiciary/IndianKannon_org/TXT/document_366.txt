Allahabad High Court
Jagesh And Others vs State Of U.P. & Another on 21 July, 2010
Court No. - 54

Case :- APPLICATION U/S 482 No. - 23405 of 2010

Petitioner :- Jagesh And Others
Respondent :- State Of U.P. & Another
Petitioner Counsel :- Suneel Rai
Respondent Counsel :- Govt. Advocate

Hon'ble Vinod Prasad,J.
Heard learned counsel for the applicants and learned AGA. The applicants must raise their grievance before the trial court for clubbing of the cases which has arises between the same parties, same nature of the offence before approaching this Court. This application is dismissed accordingly. Order Date :- 21.7.2010 Sharad
