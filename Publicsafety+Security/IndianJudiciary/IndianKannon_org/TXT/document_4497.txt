Central Government Act
Section 4 in The Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute For Medical Sciences And Technology, Trivandrum, Act, 1980
4. Incorporation of institute. The Sree Chitra Tirunal Medical Centre Society for Advanced Studies in Specialities, Trivandrum, is hereby constituted a body corporate by the name of Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute for Medical Sciences and Technology, Trivandrum, and as such body corporate, it shall have perpetual succession and a common seal with power, subject to the provisions of this Act, to acquire, hold and dispose of property and to contract, and shall, by that name, sue and be sued.
Composition of institute.
