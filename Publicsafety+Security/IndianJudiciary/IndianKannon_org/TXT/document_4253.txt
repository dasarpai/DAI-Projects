Allahabad High Court
Prem Shanker Agrawal vs U.P.Power Corp. Ltd. Lko.Through ... on 2 July, 2010
Court No. - 2

Case :- SERVICE BENCH No. - 887 of 2010

Petitioner :- Prem Shanker Agrawal
Respondent :- U.P.Power Corp. Ltd. Lko.Through Its Under Secy. And Ors.
Petitioner Counsel :- Narendra Kumar Singh,Rajat Bansal

Hon'ble Uma Nath Singh,J.
Hon'ble Devendra Kumar Arora,J.
As the earlier writ petition wherein stay had been granted on 10.01.2006 was dismissed as not pressed and further that learned counsel for petitioner was not in position to point out as to whether any leave was left in his account, for the purpose of regularisation of his service with pay, learned counsel for petitioner prays for and is permitted to withdraw this writ petition.
Hence, dismissed as withdrawn.
Order Date :- 2.7.2010 Suresh/-
