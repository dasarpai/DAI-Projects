> Title: Introduction of the Central Universities (Conditions of Service of Non-Teaching Staff) Bill, 2010.
SHRI JAGDAMBIKA PAL (DOMARIYAGANJ): Sir, I beg to move for leave to introduce a Bill to provide for uniform conditions of service for the non-teaching staff of the Central Universities and for matters connected therewith.
MR. CHAIRMAN : The question is:
“That leave be granted to introduce a Bill to provide for uniform conditions of service for the non-teaching staff of the Central Universities and for matters connected therewith.”   The motion was adopted.
  SHRI JAGDAMBIKA PAL : Sir, I introduce the Bill.
