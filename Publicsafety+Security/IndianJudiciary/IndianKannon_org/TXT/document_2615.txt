ORAL ORDER
1. Present application has been filed by the applicant-accused for grant of regular bail under Section 439 of Criminal Procedure Code.
2. The applicant accused is charged with having committed offences punishable under Sections 120B, 465, 467, 468, 471 of the IPC as well as Section 25(1)B, A of the Arms Act, for which, FIR being Complaint No. I 17 of 2010 has been registered with DCB Police Station, Ahmedabad.
3. After arguing for sometime, Mr PM Raval, learned advocate for the applicant seeks permission to withdraw this application.
4. Permission is granted. The application stands disposed of as withdrawn. Rule is discharged.
