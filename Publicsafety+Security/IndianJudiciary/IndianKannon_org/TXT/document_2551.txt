Allahabad High Court
Shyamta Prasad vs State Of U.P. & Others on 27 January, 2010
Court No. - 24

Case :- REVIEW PETITION No. - 111 of 1987

Petitioner :- Shyamta Prasad
Respondent :- State Of U.P. & Others
Petitioner Counsel :- R P Pandey
Respondent Counsel :- C S C

Hon'ble Rajiv Sharma,J.
List is being revised.
Neither learned Counsel for the petitioner is present nor there is any request for passing over or adjournment of the case. Heard learned Standing Counsel and perused the record. On perusal of the records, I am of the opinion that no good ground for reviewing the order and judgment dated 1.12.1987 is made out. Accordingly, review petition is dismissed. Order Date :- 27.1.2010 Ajit/-
