                            JUDGMENT
Kurian Joseph,J.
The writ petition is filed with the following prayer:
" Issue a writ of mandamus or any other appropriate direction or order direction the respondents 1 to 3, to render necessary police protection to the petitioner to do the work of filing the 34.250 cents lands in Survey No.769/5B of Ramamangalam Village".
2. We find that there is clearance for the same from the RDO, Muvattupuzha as seen from Exts.P3 and P4. There shall not be any obstruction for the construction activity. In the unlikely event of such obstruction, police shall render necessary assistance. The writ petition is disposed of as above.
(KURIAN JOSEPH, JUDGE) (C.T.RAVIKUMAR, JUDGE) ssn
