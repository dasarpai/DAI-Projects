Tripura High Court
Mr. Pk Dhar vs None on 17 February, 2022
                  HIGH COURT OF TRIPURA
                         AGARTALA
                     Review Pet. 8 /2022
For the Petitioner(s)    :      Mr. PK Dhar, Sr. Advocate
                                Mr. RG Chakraborty, Advocate
For Respondent(s)        :      None
HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ARINDAM LODH Order 17/02/2022 Heard Mr. PK Dhar, learned senior counsel assisted by Mr. RG Chakraborty, learned counsel appearing on behalf of the petitioner. Mr. Dhar, learned senior counsel has submitted that the court should give liberty to the petitioner to take some appropriate legal action in accordance with law.
Without any observation over the said submission, I am dismissing the said review petition. Accordingly, the instant review petition stands dismissed.
JUDGE Sanjoy
