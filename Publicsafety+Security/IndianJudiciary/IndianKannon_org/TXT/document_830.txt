ORAL
ORDER
(Per : HONOURABLE MR.JUSTICE RAVI R.TRIPATHI) Learned APP Mr.Shah makes available for perusal communication dated 11.03.2011 addressed by the Office of the Superintendent of Police, Vadodara Rural to the Office of the learned Government Pleader, High Court of Gujarat. The communication is enclosed with the following statements:
(i) Statement of the Deputy Superintendent of Police, Vadodara Division,
(ii) Statement of Jayshreebehen Maheshkumar Amritlal, wife of the convict, and
(iii) Statement of the Police Sub Inspector, Desar Police Station.
2. Taking into consideration the contents of the statements, more particularly of the wife, namely, Jayshreebehen Maheshkumar Amritlal, the Court finds no reason to grant temporary bail to the present applicant- convict. The application is dismissed. Rule is discharged.
