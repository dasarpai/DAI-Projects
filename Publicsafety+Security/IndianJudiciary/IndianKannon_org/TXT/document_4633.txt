5. 1.7.2008. Cr. Misc.No. 1611 of 2008:
Heard the counsel for the petitioner and counsel appearing on behalf of the State.
The petitioner is an accused in a case under Sections 21 and 22 of the N.D.P.S. Act.
A recovery of one and half Kilograms of 'Ganja" was made from the petitioner. The petitioner has remained in custody since 27.7.2007.
Considering the quantity of recovery made from the petitioner, I am not inclined to grant bail to the petitioner.
The bail petition of the petitioner is rejected. Cr.Misc. No. 19838 of 2008:
Mr.Umesh Chandra Verma learned counsel appearing on behalf of the petitioner seeks permission to withdraw the bail application.
Permission is allowed.
The bail application is dismissed as withdrawn.
           U.K.                                                         (Sheema Ali Khan,J)
