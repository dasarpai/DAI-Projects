When the matter was called out learned AGP Mr. Pranav Dave requested for time to take instructions. Learned Sr. Counsel Mr. P.M. Thakkar stated that the matter has been repeatedly adjourned and if time is granted, some protection or interim order may be passed or they may stay their hands.
