Allahabad High Court
Khushboo Pathak vs Orix Auto Infra Structure Service ... on 5 July, 2010
Court No. - 36

Case :- WRIT - C No. - 38326 of 2010

Petitioner :- Khushboo Pathak
Respondent :- Orix Auto Infra Structure Service Ltd.
Petitioner Counsel :- Atul Kumar

Hon'ble Sheo Kumar Singh,J.
Hon'ble Rajesh Chandra,J.
This is a writ petition against the private financer against whom the writ petition is not maintainable. In view of the aforesaid, writ petition fails and is dismissed. However, it will be open for the petitioner to avail the remedy which is available to him in accordance with law. Order Date :- 5.7.2010 Sachdeva
