Allahabad High Court
Ravendra Singh vs Union Of India And Others on 1 February, 2010
Court No. - 54

Case :- APPLICATION U/S 482 No. - 869 of 2010

Petitioner :- Ravendra Singh
Respondent :- Union Of India And Others
Petitioner Counsel :- Pawan Kumar Dubey
Respondent Counsel :- Sanjay Kumar Singh

Hon'ble Ravindra Singh,J.
Heard the learned counsel for the applicant, learned A.G.A. and Sri Sanjay Kumar, Special Public Prosecutor, is also present. It is contended by the learned counsel for the applicant that the District Opium Officer did not express any type of suspicion either oral or in writing at the time when the opium was weight and examined by him. In respect of the similar case proceedings against O.P. has been stayed in criminal misc. application no. 279 of 2008. The Special Public Prosecutor may file counter affidavit within four weeks from today. It shall be informed by the learned counsel for the applicant to him in writing. Consider the submission the further proceedings of complaint case no. 13of 2006 under section 8/19 and 18/26 of N.D.P.S. Act pending in the court of Additional Sessions Judge/F.T.C. No.1, Shahjahanpur, shall remain stayed till the next date of listing.
List after tour weeks.
Order Date :- 1.2.2010 N.A.
