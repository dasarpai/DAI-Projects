Allahabad High Court
Ram Rahis And Others vs State Of U.P. on 13 July, 2010
Court No. - 53

Case :- APPLICATION U/S 482 No. - 23695 of 2008

Petitioner :- Ram Rahis And Others
Respondent :- State Of U.P.
Petitioner Counsel :- Sanjay Dubey
Respondent Counsel :- Govt. Advocate

Hon'ble Ashwani Kumar Singh,J.
The list is being revised. None appeared on behalf of the applicant to press this application. Sri B.K.Ojha, Advocate is present on behalf of opposite party. The petition is dismissed for non-prosecution. Interim order, if any, is vacated.
The Registrar of this Court shall send a copy of this order to the District & Sessions Judge concerned, who shall apprise the Court concerned of this order and the court concerned shall proceed, in accordance with law.
Order Date :- 13.7.2010 kvg/-
