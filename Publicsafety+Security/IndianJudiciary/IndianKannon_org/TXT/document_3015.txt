Allahabad High Court
Binda Prasad vs A.K. Gupta, Chief Engg. Allahabad ... on 23 July, 2010
Court No. - 10

Case :- CONTEMPT APPLICATION (CIVIL) No. - 3424 of 2010

Petitioner :- Binda Prasad
Respondent :- A.K. Gupta, Chief Engg. Allahabad Zone P.W.D.Allahabad &
Ors
Petitioner Counsel :- B.L. Verma

Hon'ble Vikram Nath,J.
It is alleged that the order dated 29.03.2010 passed by this court has been violated. From a perusal of the petition, a prima facie case is made out. Issue notice to opposite parties fixing 7th September, 2010. The opposite parties need not appear at this stage.
The Counter affidavit may be filed within the aforesaid period or else charges may be framed after summoning the noticee.
However, one more opportunity is granted to the opposite parties to comply with the order within a month.
The office may send a copy of this order along with the notice. Order Date :- 23.7.2010 SS
