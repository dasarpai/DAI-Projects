O R D E R
Today, when this writ petition was taken up for hearing, there is no appearance for the petitioner, either in person or through his counsel. Since the writ petition is of the year 2004, this Court is constrained to dismiss this writ petition for non prosecution. Accordingly, the writ petition is dismissed for non prosecution. No costs.
