Patna High Court - Orders
Ramchandra Rai vs The State Of Bihar & Ors on 8 September, 2011
                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                          Cr.Misc. No.30316 of 2011
                                Ramchandra Rai
                                    Versus
                           The State Of Bihar & Ors
                                 -----------
02. 08.09.2011 Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and the State.
Considering the submissions and the grounds stated in the petition, the connected writ application vide Cr. W.J.C. No. 1138 of 2010 which was dismissed for non-prosecution, is restored to its original file.
Accordingly, the application stands disposed of.
Jagdish/- ( Shailesh Kumar Sinha,J.)
