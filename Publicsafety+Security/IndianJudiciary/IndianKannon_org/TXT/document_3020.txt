                                        ORAL ORDER
2 01-06-2020 In the present case, the petitioner is seeking relief for payment of GPF amount for the period from 1970 to 1983 as has been submitted that this period has not been taken into consideration for calculation of the amount of gratuity, further he makes a prayer that he should be granted the benefit of pension and gratuity, which cannot be given to him in the present proceeding in view of the letter no. 1835 dated 04.10.2017 issued by the Collector, Gopalganj whereby he has held that as the criminal proceeding is going on, the petitioner is not entitled to any benefit under the heading of pension and gratuity, whereupon counsel for the petitioner makes a prayer that he should be granted some time to challenge the aforesaid letter dated 04.10.2017 by filing an appropriate application.
As prayed for, let this case be listed after six weeks.
(Shivaji Pandey, J) V.K.Pandey/-
