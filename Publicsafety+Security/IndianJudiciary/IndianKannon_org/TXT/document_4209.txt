Patna High Court - Orders
Taslim Dhunia &Amp; Anr vs State Of Bihar on 29 October, 2010
                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                        Cr.Misc. No.14985 of 2009
                              TASLIM DHUNIA & ANR
                                     Versus
                                 STATE OF BIHAR
                                    -------
02. 29.10.2010 In spite of repeated calls and waiting for a reasonable time, there is no representation on behalf of learned counsel for the petitioners to press this application. Learned Additional Public Prosecutor is present.
Accordingly, this application stands dismissed for non-prosecution. Rajeev/ (Akhilesh Chandra, J.)
