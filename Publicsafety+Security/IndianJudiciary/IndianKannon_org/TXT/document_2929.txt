Central Information Commission
Mr. K.P. Singh vs Mtnl on 23 September, 2009
              Central Information Commission
                                                            CIC/AD/A/2009/001064

                                                          Dated: 23 September 2009


Name of the Applicant                    :      MR. K.P. SINGH


Name of the Public Authority             :      MTNL


Background
1 The Applicant filed an RTI application dated 30.10.08 with the CPIO/ MTNL, New Delhi, seeking information against 8 points related to the SC/ST Telecom Employees Welfare Association including its present status, audit report of the Association etc. The CPIO replied on 16.12.08 intimating the Applicant that his application is under process and the reply is awaited from the concerned unit. On not receiving any further reply from the CPIO, the Applicant filed a First Appeal before the Appellate Authority on 05.12.08. On not receiving any reply from by the First Appellate Authority, the Applicant filed a Second Appeal before the CIC on 15.04.09.
2. The Bench of Mrs. Annapurna Dixit, Information Commissioner, scheduled the hearing for 23rd September, 2009.
3. Mr. Parmatma Rai, DGM(RTI & PG), Mr. Sanjeev Kerketta, Asst. MGR(HR), Mr. S.S Kaushal, MGR and Mr. Pravin Punj, GM(BD)& CPIO represented the Public Information.
4. The Applicant was heard through audio conference during the hearing.
Decision
5. The Appellant informed the Commission that he had received the information sent vide letter dated 18.9.09 and that he is satisfied with it.
6. The case is accordingly closed.
