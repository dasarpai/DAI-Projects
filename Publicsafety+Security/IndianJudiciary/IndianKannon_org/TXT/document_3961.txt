                                           ORAL ORDER
(Per: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE MANDHATA SINGH) 3 08-09-2011 Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned counsel for the State.
Ligature mark found all around the neck is to show homicidal death. If really pressing of neck was not seen by the informant's son who was residing with deceased then also circumstance is involving petitioner and her husband in killing.
Considering the facts and circumstances of the case, prayer of the petitioner for bail is rejected.
(Mandhata Singh, J) Shailendra Bhushan Prasad/-
