Patna High Court - Orders
Narain Prasad Mehta vs Madan Mohan Thakur &Amp; Ors on 21 September, 2010
                  IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT PATNA
                                  SA No.320 of 2009
                               NARAIN PRASAD MEHTA
                                         Versus
                           MADAN MOHAN THAKUR & ORS
                                       -----------
4. 21.9.2010. Office has placed this file for hearing under Order XLI Rule 11 of the Code of Civil Procedure without pointing out as to whether the defects as per stamp report dated 18.1.2010 have been removed or not in view of the order dated 24.2.2010 passed by the Lawazima Board.
Let the office put up this matter after ascertaining the same.
( Dr. Ravi Ranjan, J) P.S.
