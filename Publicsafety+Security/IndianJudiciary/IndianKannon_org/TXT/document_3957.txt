N.N.Nigam vs Life Insurance Corporation Of ... on 9 July, 2010
Court No. - 38

Case :- WRIT - A No. - 6837 of 1993

Respondent :- Life Insurance Corporation Of India & Another

Hon'ble Shishir Kumar,J.
This case is listed under the Heading 'Old Cases'. In spite of repeated calls, no one appears to press this writ petition on merits.
Sri R.P. Goel on behalf of the respondents is present. Therefore, the writ petition is hereby dismissed. Interim order, if any, stands discharged. No order is passed as to costs. Order Date :- 9.7.2010 V.Sri/-
