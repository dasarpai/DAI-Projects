Central Government Act
Section 50 in The Unlawful Activities (Prevention) Amendment Act, 2004
50. Saving.- Nothing in this Act shall affect the jurisdiction exercisable by, or the procedure applicable to, any court or other authority under any law relating to the navy, army or air force or other armed forces of the Union.
