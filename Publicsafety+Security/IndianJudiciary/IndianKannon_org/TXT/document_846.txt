Central Government Act
Section 9(6) in THE APPRENTICES ACT, 1961
(6) In any such training institute, which shall be located within the premises of the most suitable establishment in the locality or at any other convenient place 36 [the trade apprentices] engaged by two or more employers may be imparted basic training.
