Central Government Act
Section 308(2) in The New Delhi Municipal Council Act. 1994 (As Passed By The Houses Of Parliament)
(2) Every person shall take all reasonable means to prevent every child under the age of twelve years being in his charge from casing himself in any public street or public place.
