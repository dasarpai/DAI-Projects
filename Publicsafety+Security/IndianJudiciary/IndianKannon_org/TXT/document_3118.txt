Central Government Act
Section 23 in The Securities and Exchange Board of India Act, 1992
23. Protection of action taken in good faith.—No suit, prosecu­tion or other legal proceedings shall lie against the Central Government 1[or Board] or any officer of the Central Government or any member, officer or other employee of the Board for any­thing which is in good faith done or intended to be done under this Act or the rules or regulations made thereunder.
