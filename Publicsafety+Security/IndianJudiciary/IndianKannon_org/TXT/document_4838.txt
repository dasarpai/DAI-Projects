Allahabad High Court
Abhay Kumar Singh vs State Of U.P. & Another on 18 January, 2010
Court No. - 21

Case :- WRIT - C No. - 1942 of 2010

Petitioner :- Abhay Kumar Singh
Respondent :- State Of U.P. & Another
Petitioner Counsel :- Mahesh Chandra Rai,Kavindra Singh
Respondent Counsel :- C.S.C.

Hon'ble V.K. Shukla,J.
Grievance of the petitioner is that he has moved application for grant of fire arm licence on 1.7.2006. Petitioner submits that till date no endeavour has been taken by the authority concerned to decide the same.
Consequently, Licensing Authority/ District Magistrate of the concern district is directed to see and ensure that final decision is taken in accordance with law preferably within period of three months from the date of production of certified copy of this order. Whatever decision is taken, same be communicated to the petitioner. With these observation, writ petition is disposed of. Order Date :- 18.1.2010 T.S.
