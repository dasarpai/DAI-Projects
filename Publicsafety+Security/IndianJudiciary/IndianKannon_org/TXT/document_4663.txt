Central Government Act
Section 11(b) in The Banking Companies (Acquisition And Transfer Of Undertakings) And Financial Institutions Laws (Amendment) Act, 2006
(b) after sub- section (2), the following sub- section shall be inserted, namely:-" (3) Nothing contained in this section shall apply during the period for which the Board of Directors of a corresponding new bank had been superseded under sub- section (1) of section 18A: Provided that the Administrator may, if he considers it appropriate in the interest of the corresponding new bank whose Board of Directors had been superseded, call annual general meeting in accordance with the provisions of this section.".
