Central Government Act
Section 22 in The Extradition Act, 1962
22. Liability of fugitive criminals to be arrested and surrendered or returned.—Every fugitive criminal of a foreign State 1[***] shall, subject to the provisions of this Act, be liable be arrested and surrendered or returned, whether the offence in respect of which the surrender or return is sought was committed before or after the commencement of this Act, and whether or not a court in India has jurisdiction to try that offence.—Every fugitive criminal of a foreign State 1[***] shall, subject to the provisions of this Act, be liable be arrested and surrendered or returned, whether the offence in respect of which the surrender or return is sought was committed before or after the commencement of this Act, and whether or not a court in India has jurisdiction to try that offence."
